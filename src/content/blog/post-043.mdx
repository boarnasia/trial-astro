---
title: 'CSS Scroll Snap'
description: 'Learn CSS Scroll Snap to create precise, polished scroll experiences without JavaScript.'
pubDate: 'Jul 14 2023'
---

CSS Scroll Snap gives you precise control over scroll positions, ensuring the viewport always stops at meaningful content boundaries. It works entirely in CSS without any JavaScript, making it performant and simple to implement.

The scroll-snap-type property on the scroll container defines the axis and strictness of snapping. scroll-snap-align on each item defines the snap point — start, center, or end. These two properties are all you need for most use cases.

Scroll Snap is perfect for carousels, image galleries, and full-page sections. The CSS-only approach means it works on mobile devices with momentum scrolling, and the browser handles all the physics of snapping after a touch gesture ends.
