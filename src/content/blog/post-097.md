title: 'Productivity Tools for Developers'
description: 'A curated list of tools, extensions, and workflows to boost productivity for software developers.'
pubDate: 'Aug 14 2023'
---

Productivity for developers is less about working faster and less about eliminating the friction between intention and execution. The most valuable investments reduce the cost of context switching and interrupted flow.

Keyboard-centric workflows — mastering editor shortcuts, using a keyboard-driven browser, and learning command-line tools — dramatically reduce the time spent on navigation and repetitive tasks. Every interaction that requires reaching for the mouse interrupts typing flow.

Regular time blocking protects deep work from meetings and interruptions. Batching communication — checking email and messages at scheduled times rather than continuously — reduces context switching. The goal is longer uninterrupted periods where you can work on complex problems.
